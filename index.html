Why My Toddler is a Terrorist

Screaming: Tantrum City

Many are familiar with the term "the terrible twos." Well, no mere words can convey just how terrible they can be. Just... full on meltdowns about NOTHING. The wailing. The tears. The embarrasment when it happens out in public, even though we shouldn't be embarrassed because let's be serious, you're kids have meltdowns, too! My personal favorite: going to pick her up and as soon as I touch her she magically turn into a marshmallow and collapses on the floor, making it look like I literally just yeeted her forcefully into the ground. Let's not talk about the fact that she screams when happy too...



The Never-Ending Snackscapade

She literally always wants snacks. But not THAT snack. Seems sus.



Sleeping... Or Lack Thereof

Two words: sleep regressions. No fun. Hard pass. I wish I could just say hard pass. Multiple wakings in the middle of the night. Refusing sleep until 10-11 pm. Waking up at 5 am. Hating naps. I think she's using sleep deprivation as a reprogramming tactic so I'll give her all of the cookies. Send help.



So Many Stamps

She loves stamps. But not washable stamps. My permanant address stamper. All over the floor. All over her shirt. On her foot. Terrorist.



So Many Bad Decisions

She makes so many decisions that would in some way, shape, or form unalive her, even though it's my express duty to keep her alive. Standing on bikes. Pulling dogs' tails. Diving headfirst off of furniture. Why are you like this!?
